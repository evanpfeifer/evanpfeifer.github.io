Golf Has an Image Problem

SEPTEMBER 26, 2020

If you know me, you probably know that I love golf. However, the longevity of this sacred game is in danger: less people are choosing to pick up golf. To turn things around, the "old, rich, and white" stereotype associated with the game has got to go. It's in the hands of golfers worldwide to help golf save face.
